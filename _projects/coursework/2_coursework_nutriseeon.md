#### [Key Concept]
NutriSeeON will act as the eye of blind users, helping them to shop healthy groceries.
- Real-time Nutrition Facts Extraction by
    -  MobileCamera
    - Tactile/AuditoryFeedback
    - DeeplearningbasedImageprocessing
